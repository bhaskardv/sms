About Biarca

We Help IT Enable Cloud Computing in the Enterprise

The world of enterprise IT is in dramatic transition. Cloud computing is redefining the way IT services are delivered at every level from how applications are developed and deployed, to the underlying software and hardware infrastructure layers. Cloud Computing is both an opportunity and a threat to every business. Agility and operational efficiency are paramount. IT organizations have to meet the primary needs of the business, and also find ways to reimagine the organization and infrastructure to meet new market and operational demands.

Some organizations are turning to Public Clouds to deliver agility, flexibility and scalability. In this model, there is no need for up-front investment, and you pay as you go. The down side is that the on-going expenses can exceed the cost of on premise infrastructure within a relatively short period. Users must also relinquish control over where data resides, and peak hour usage can have performance and outage ramifications.

A private cloud that is designed around "Open" standards and technologies can provide all the benefits of cloud computing without the downside risks. In this context, a public cloud could supplement the private cloud resources for cloud bursting.

Biarca can help. We are a leading edge Professional services company that helps enterprises transform IT processes and infrastructures into strategic advantage. Our services help organizations architect and deploy private cloud implementations designed around leading Open Source cloud platforms like OpenStack, Cloud Foundry and Docker / Containers and Hadoop.

Biarca offers a collaborative approach to helping IT organizations strategically prioritize and implement IT initiatives so that IT operations will be more agile, and efficient. We help maintain existing investments in line of business applications while modernizing and aligning them to meet the needs for today and the future. We harness the power of the leading Cloud Open Source software platforms best ideas, practices and proven methodologies and apply them for our customers' benefit.
